I am Andrej, a Communication Designer graduated from Politecnico di Milano, in Italy.

Throughout my journey in communication design, I've worked on many different projects, from 2D graphics to detailed 3D visualizations. Educated at the Politecnico di Milano, my studies paired perfectly with practical experience, which I gained while freelancing during my academic years and further deepened during my two-year tenure in a communication agency.

My proficiency with the Adobe Suite, particularly tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere, After Effects, Lightroom, and XD, has allowed me to refine my design and visual storytelling skills.

Beyond graphical expertise, I possess a robust grounding in programming, boasting hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This blend of design and coding positions me as a versatile professional, primed to tackle creative challenges from various angles.
